Our Imapact by the Numbers

We proudly support over 500 Palestinian artists, showcasing more than 1,000 unique artworks. With over 10,000 monthly website visits, interest in Palestinian art is growing. Notably, 75% of our sales directly benefit the artists. Additionally, we have 20 partnerships with international and local organizations, collaboratively supporting and nurturing artistic talent in Palestine.
